Why Us ?


Islamic OS is a new project by some practicing muslims.

It is based on Ubuntu 22.04 LTS. The speciality of this OS is that you will be welcomed warmly in a Islamic way.

And the most important feature is that you can’t visit age restricted websites anymore.
